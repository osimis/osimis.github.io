Simple integration with Imaging Sites

A quick configuration enables imaging sites to automate uploads, and thereby eliminate the time consuming anonymisation and matching processes.

Alternatively, images can be loaded simply by dragging and dropping files into the Cohortis website or by identifying imaging studies on CD or Hard Drive.

Once in the cloud, you can view your images and share them quickly and easily.

Easy and fast follow-up thanks to advanced user tools

We analysed for you which parts in the process that are currently handled by email messages should in fact be handled by notifications or in-app messages. 

Never miss a trial related deadline thanks to the alerts that you can set.

Dashboards give a visual indicator of what is going right… and what is off track. Imaging Sites, CROs and Central Reading Labs each get their dedicated and personalised dashboard.

Regulation compliance throughout

With Cohortis, you have complete control and visibility into how your medical images are collected and viewed throughout your clinical trial.

Timely reporting & delivery

Reporting to sponsors becomes a matter of simply clicking a button. 

The alert and notification system that is embedded in Cohortis ensures that all actors in a trial are and stay on the same page.

With less errors and more control, your timely delivery is no longer just a dream.
