Features Spotlight.

Learn more about the powerful features and options of Cohortis

Medical device manufacturers and pharmaceutical companies recognise that medical imaging is often crucial to the clinical trial process when validating the safety and efficacy of devices and drugs. The Cohortis clinical trial image management software from Osimis powers all aspects of clinical trials including image acquisition, image management, core lab image routing, advanced workflow and task management, reporting, and much more.

Import automation from any PACS or drive

Medical imaging studies are large and can contain hundreds of files. There are 3 ways to upload or import medical imaging data into Cohortis. The most powerful way is by interfacing your PACS system with the cloud through the CohortisTrialloader. A quick configuration enables imaging sites to automate uploads, and thereby eliminate the time consuming anonymisation and matching processes. Alternatively, images can be loaded simply by dragging and dropping files into the Cohortis website or by identifying imaging studies on CD or Hard Drive. Once in the cloud, you can view your images and share them quickly and easily.

Real-Time visibility and reporting

Gain real-time visibility into all medical imaging clinical trial imaging activities including performance metrics, bottlenecks, task management activities, and more. With Cohortis, you have complete control on how your medical images are shared and viewed by others. See who has access to the images with our advanced usage reports. See every interaction with a complete audit history of all the changes that are made to each study.

Scalable & reliable performance

Leverage the full power of the cloud with Cohortis. We utilize a global infrastructure to provide our customers with a secure, geographically redundant, high performance solution that scales with your business. Take a look at our system performance and uptime stats.

Sharing made simple and secure

Cohortis gives you complete control of all your medical images and files. By default, all images are private, but you can choose to share them with anyone in a secure manner. You can share a single imaging study, or a folder with multiple studies and files. You can always see who has access to what images and files, and you can revoke access at any time.

Compliant data capture

The Cohortis clinical trial management system is Title 21 CFR Part 11 compliant. Our electronic data capture system conforms to all regulatory requirements involving audit trails, security features, e-signatures, and disaster recovery. With Cohortis, you have complete control and visibility into how your medical images are collected and viewed throughout your clinical trial. See who has access to the images. Get complete traceability and audit history for your clinical trial imaging endpoints.

Highest security standards and risk mitigation

Our system permissions ensure that only those users who have been granted access to a study, can view that study. All data is stored in HIPAA compliant data centers to ensure safety and security. Manage all clinical trial imaging activities on a single platform, providing you with the highest level of control of and access to your data and other clinical resources.

Compatibility and interoperability all over

The Cohortis medical image viewer is built to work entirely within a modern web browser, with no plugins or other proprietary technology. This means it works on most operating systems, mobile devices, and smartphones, including Mac OS X, Windows, iOS, and Android. Here's a full list of the supported browsers and platforms: Browser support - Chrome 16 and above, Safari 6 and above, Firefox 14 and above, IE 10 and above. Platform support - Windows 7 and above, Mac OS X 10.6 and above.

Get Setup in Minutes

Sign up for a free trial and immediately test out our medical image viewer with a sample imaging study. Then go ahead and upload your own studies from a CD, your hard drive, or a PACS system.
